About Me

I'm Silviu, I'm 27 years old and I love to program. I like creating things in many different languages, but I originally started programming in QBasic as a teenager, going through game development tutorials.

I have an Associate's degree in Computer Programming from Mesa Community College as well as certificates in Web Development and in Programming & Systems Analysis from there. I graduated with honors (3.7 GPA). The technologies I know best are Java, Javascript, HTML5/CSS3, MySQL, and Git but I am also familiar with the basics of Python, C#, Ruby, Node.js and a few others, and I enjoy learning new technologies. Currently I am working on learning React.

I am also familiar with and can implement common data structures and algorithms. At my last job I worked as a software tester/QA for about a year. The experience was fun and has given me an eye for finding bugs in software and made me realize the importance of test driven development, but I am looking to find a development role, preferably full-stack development.

In my spare time when I'm not programming I like to relax by playing games, exercising or listening to music.
